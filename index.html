📏 Okul Kuralları / School Rules

💡 "must" = -meli/-malı (zorunluluk) | "mustn't" = yapmamalı (yasak)

Okuma / Reading

My School – Okuma Metni
Hi! My name is Elif. I am a student at Atatürk Middle School in Ankara. My school is big and beautiful. There is a library, a canteen, a gym and a laboratory in my school.

My favourite place is the library. I love reading books there. My teacher is very kind. Her name is Mrs Filiz. She teaches English.

At our school, students must wear a uniform and be on time. We mustn't run in the corridor. I love my school!
📝 Metni okuduktan sonra soruları cevapla / After reading, answer the questions:
1. Where is Elif's school?
2. What is Elif's favourite place?
3. What must students wear?
1. Ankara
2. The library
3. A uniform

My name is Ahmet. Let me tell you about my day. I wake up at 6:30 every morning. First, I brush my teeth and then I have breakfast. I always eat eggs and drink tea.

I go to school at 7:45. School finishes at 3:30. After school, I do my homework. In the evening, I sometimes watch TV with my family. I go to bed at 10 o'clock. I have a great daily routine! 😊
1. What time does Ahmet wake up?

💡 Like / Love / Hate + -ing

💡 like = sevmek | love = çok sevmek | hate = nefret etmek. Bunların ardından fiil -ing alır!
YapıÖrnek
like + -ingI like playing football.
love + -ingShe loves listening to music.
hate + -ingHe hates reading comics.
I love going cycling on weekends.
Hafta sonları bisiklete binmeyi çok seviyorum.
📄

Hi, I'm Zeynep! I love telling people about my hobbies. In my free time, I love listening to music. I like painting pictures too. My favourite painter is Van Gogh.

My brother loves playing football. He practises three times a week. My sister likes surfing the internet and watching videos. She wants to be a YouTuber one day! 😄

We all hate doing the dishes. But we love spending time together as a family.
1. What does Zeynep love doing?
2. How often does her brother practise?
3. What does the whole family hate?
1. Listening to music
2. Three times a week
3. Doing the dishes

📝 Comparative Adjectives (Karşılaştırmalı Sıfatlar)

💡 İki şeyi kıyaslamak için kullanılır. Kısa sıfatlara -er eklenir, uzun sıfatların önüne "more" gelir!
SıfatKarşılaştırmaÖrnek
kindkinderShe is kinder than me.
honestmore honestHe is more honest.
goodbetterShe is better than him.
My best friend is more supportive than anyone I know.
En iyi arkadaşım, tanıdıklarımın hepsinden daha destekleyici.
📄

Okuma / Reading

True friendship is one of the greatest things in life. A good friend is loyal, honest and caring. But what makes a friend better than others?

My best friend Yasemin is more reliable than anyone I know. She always keeps her promises. She is also kinder than me — she helps everyone without asking for anything in return.

I think a trustworthy friend is more valuable than gold. I am lucky to have Yasemin in my life. True friends are harder to find than you think! 💛
1. What qualities does a good friend have?
2. Why is Yasemin more reliable?
3. What does the writer compare to gold?
1. Loyal, honest, caring
2. She always keeps her promises
3. A trustworthy friend
✏️

📝 If Clause Type 1 (1. Tip Koşul Cümlesi)

💡 Gerçekleşmesi mümkün koşullar için kullanılır: If + Present Simple → will + fiil
If (Koşul)Sonuç
If you study hard,you will pass the exam.
If it rains,we will stay at home.
If she exercises,she will be healthier.
📄

Okuma / Reading

Being a teenager today is not easy. We face a lot of pressure from school, family and social media. But there are ways to have a healthy and happy teen life.

First, if you eat a balanced diet and exercise regularly, you will feel better both physically and mentally. Second, if you limit your screen time, you will have more time for real-life activities.

If you volunteer in your community, you will become more self-confident and make new friends. Remember: if you believe in yourself, anything is possible! 💪
1. What happens if teenagers eat well and exercise?
2. What does limiting screen time lead to?
3. How can volunteering help?
1. They will feel better
2. More time for real activities
3. More self-confident and new friends
