📏 Must / Mustn't

💡 must = -meli/-malı (zorunluluk) | mustn't = yapmamalı (yasak)
Must ✅Mustn't 🚫
You must be on time.You mustn't run.
You must wear uniform.You mustn't shout.
Students must keep the library quiet.
Öğrenciler kütüphaneyi sessiz tutmalı.
You mustn't use phones in class.
Sınıfta telefon kullanmamalısın.
📄

Okuma / Reading

My School
Hi! My name is Elif. I am a student at Atatürk Middle School in Ankara. My school is big and beautiful. There is a library, a canteen, a gym and a laboratory.

My favourite place is the library. My teacher's name is Mrs Filiz. She teaches English. Students must wear a uniform and be on time. We mustn't run in the corridor. I love my school!
1. Where is Elif's school?  2. What is her favourite place?  3. What must students wear?
1. Ankara   2. The library   3. A uniform

I love our Sunday breakfasts! 🍊🥚🧀

Friendship & Comparative Adjectives
UNIT 1

SıfatKarşılaştırmaÖrnek

Okuma / Reading

True friendship is one of the greatest things in life. A good friend is loyal, honest and caring.

My best friend Yasemin is more reliable than anyone I know. She always keeps her promises. She is also kinder than me — she helps everyone without asking for anything in return.

I think a trustworthy friend is more valuable than gold. True friends are harder to find than you think! 💛
1. What qualities does a good friend have?  2. Why is Yasemin reliable?  3. What is more valuable than gold?
1. Loyal, honest, caring   2. She keeps her promises   3. A trustworthy friend

If (Present Simple)Sonuç (will)
If you study hard,you will pass the exam.

Okuma / Reading

Being a teenager today is not easy. We face a lot of pressure from school, family and social media.

If you eat a balanced diet and exercise regularly, you will feel better. If you limit your screen time, you will have more time for real activities.

If you volunteer in your community, you will become more self-confident. If you believe in yourself, anything is possible! 💪
1. What happens if you eat well?  2. What does limiting screen time lead to?  3. How can volunteering help?
1. You will feel better   2. More time for real activities   3. More self-confident
